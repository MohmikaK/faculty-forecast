Our Purpose

Colleges and universities frequently experience difficulty anticipating faculty vacancies and determining when to initiate the recruiting process which results in compromising the quality of education and burdening existing faculty with increased workload. Faculty Forecast empowers colleges and universities to make data-driven decisions when it comes to faculty recruitment. By accurately predicting faculty turnover and providing an ultimatum for initiating the hiring process, our portal ensures timely recruitment, ultimately enhancing academic excellence.
